Frequently Asked Questions

01

What exactly does a Technology Solutions Provider do?

I help businesses prepare their infrastructure for AI workloads, modernize their operations, and get more out of their technology. That means assessing your current systems, designing architecture that supports growth, and often building or implementing the solution alongside your team.

02

We already have a DevOps/IT team. Why would we need you?

Your team runs your systems day-to-day. I come in for specific challenges — migrating to the cloud, preparing for AI adoption, redesigning a network, or breaking through a technical bottleneck. I work with your team, not instead of them.

03

How do engagements typically work?

Most start with a free discovery call to understand your situation. From there, it might be a focused assessment (1–2 weeks), a strategy engagement (3–4 weeks), or ongoing implementation support. Scope and timeline depend on your needs.

04

Do you only work with large enterprises?

No. Most of my clients are small-to-medium businesses and startups. The common thread is that they're building something technical and need expertise they don't have in-house. Enterprise teams are welcome too — the fundamentals are the same.

05

Do you also build websites and set up CRM/analytics tools?

Yes. I build custom websites with SEO optimization, set up and train teams on CRM platforms, and configure advanced analytics dashboards. These services pair well with infrastructure work — getting your digital presence and internal tooling right alongside your technical foundation.

06

What industries do you work with?

Infrastructure challenges are cross-industry. I currently serve as Solutions Architect in the security and solar industry, and I've worked across tech startups, e-commerce, and professional services. If your business runs on technology, the fundamentals apply.

07

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope. Assessments and strategy engagements are project-based with clear deliverables. Implementation support is billed on a weekly or monthly basis. The discovery call is free — we'll figure out the right scope together.

08

Can we start with a small engagement to test the fit?

Absolutely. Many clients start with an assessment or a focused sprint. If it works, we expand. No long-term contracts required.
