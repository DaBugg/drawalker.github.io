About

Hey, I'm David W.

I'm an AI & Infrastructure Advisor and Solutions Architect with 10+ years of experience across network engineering, cloud architecture, software development, and business operations. I help businesses bridge the gap between where they are and where technology can take them.


I started my career as a Network Engineer, building and managing enterprise networks from the ground up. Today I work as a Solutions Architect for the largest SIM and solar security systems provider in Florida, where I've completely overhauled business operations — from infrastructure and internal tooling to workflow automation using AI. I've seen firsthand how the right technology decisions can transform how an entire company operates.


My background spans the full stack — designing physical networks with Cisco, UniFi, and Juniper; deploying on GCP and AWS; automating workflows with Python; building production web applications with the MERN stack; implementing CRM systems and advanced analytics platforms; and leveraging AI to streamline operations and decision-making. I've also built and deployed 15+ responsive websites with SEO optimization and conversion-focused design for small businesses and startups.


Outside of advisory work, I practice micro-soldering, design PCBs with KiCad, repair hardware, and build front-ends and back-ends for startup apps I'm working on. I like staying close to the metal while thinking about the big picture.


Get in touch to discuss how I can help your team.
